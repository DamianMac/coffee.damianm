## What is this?

Damian's Coffee Diary is an archive of my old coffee review Instagram, transplanted here for posterity.

I've been making espresso at home for years, and along the way I started documenting the specialty roasters and coffees I was trying. This is that archive.

## What do I drink?

I mostly drink espresso-based drinks — flat whites, cappuccinos, and straight shots. I have a home espresso setup and enjoy dialling in different coffees.

I have a preference for lighter to medium roasts with fruit-forward or complex flavour profiles, though I enjoy a good classic milk blend too.

## Where am I based?

Brisbane, Australia. There's a great specialty coffee scene here and in the surrounding region, and a lot of the roasters featured here are local to South East Queensland.
